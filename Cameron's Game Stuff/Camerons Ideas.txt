

3 places to tap

Forge: tap tap until you create a sword. There will be a certain number of taps need to create a sword. Once completed it will be given a random attack value.

Quest: tap tap to attack monsters and get special items needed to make special swords. The better sword you send with your knight the more likely you will recieve rare items.

Mine: tap tap to collect metals. Metals will be unlocked to be able to mine them, each tap could be like .01 or 1 of ingot. to make it harder to mine. Possible metals: Bronze, Iron, Silver, Gold, Diamond, Platinum.
